About Me & Education

Who I Am

I am a dedicated Information Systems student based in the Davao Del Norte State College, with a strong focus on building robust, scalable digital infrastructures. I specialize in bridging the gap between complex manual operations and streamlined technological solutions.

My technical foundation spans across database architecture—specifically designing intricate PostgreSQL schemas with role-based access logic—and crafting intuitive user experiences through Hybrid Design Thinking and Figma prototyping.

Currently, I am developing MetroFleet, a comprehensive digital information system designed for the Davao Metro Shuttle (DMS) context. This capstone project aims to modernize manual fleet dispatching through real-time GPS tracking, QR-based inspections, and synchronized data architecture across multiple regional branches.
